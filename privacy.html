Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how CheckMyBurnout ("we," "us," or "the Site") handles information when you use checkmyburnout.com. Privacy is central to how this tool was designed — the assessment runs entirely in your browser, and your answers are never transmitted to our servers.

Information we do not collect

The burnout assessment performs all scoring calculations locally in your browser using JavaScript. The answers you select, your scores, and your dimension breakdowns are never sent to or stored on our servers at any point. We have no record of your individual responses or results.

Local browser storage

To enable the weekly trend tracking feature, your scores are saved using your browser's local storage on your own device. This data is never transmitted anywhere and is only accessible within your own browser. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's site data for checkmyburnout.com.

Sensitive nature of this data

We recognize that burnout and mental health information is sensitive. This is precisely why we built the assessment to function entirely client-side rather than collecting any responses server-side, even anonymously. If you use a shared or public computer, we recommend clearing your browser data afterward, since local storage persists on that device.

Cookies and analytics

We may use cookies or similar technologies for basic site functionality (such as remembering your theme preference) and for aggregate, anonymized analytics about site usage (such as which pages are visited). This analytics data does not include your quiz answers or results.

Advertising — Google AdSense

This site may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You may opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ads Settings or aboutads.info.

Contact form

If you submit a message via our contact form, the information you provide is used only to respond to your inquiry and is not shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact us

Questions about this policy? Reach us via our contact page.
